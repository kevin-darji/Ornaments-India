Exhibition

Signature, Mumbai From 21st To 24th February 2014

Signature is India’s top-of-the-line jewellery show, showcasing the best jewellery collections by the country’s leading manufacturers. Restricted to about 550+ exhibitors, the focus is on product – jewellery of the highest standards of design, craftsmanship and finish. Just what you need to revitalize your inventory for the coming year.

India’s jewellery manufacturing strengths are now established as a reliable and competitive source which retailers and consumers the world over have come to appreciate. Visiting Signature is a profitable step to growing your business. The show is compact enough to fully explore sourcing opportunities and, with a large number of new participants, it is the ideal platform to develop additional contacts. It is also an opportunity to enjoy exclusive Signature ambience and hospitality.

Signature has gained prestige and stature as the show which jewellers must attend to keep abreast of the jewellery market – the new styles and collections, the price points, what’s trending and in demand. Being compact in size, the show offers jewellers the opportunity to have in-depth discussions with India’s best manufacturers and meet many new suppliers. It has also made a mark for its exclusive ambience and hospitality. No wonder, it is now considered India’s most important jewellery fair after IIJS.
